Modelling and Simulation Collaboration Webtool (MSCW) is a South African integrated collaboration-network established and hosted at the CSIR and funded by the Department of Science and Innovation (DSI). MSCW focuses on the development and application of modelling and simulations techniques in various range of sectors, academic institutions, government departments, research councils, industries, and financial institutions. We are also a resource hub, ensuring that existing knowledge is maintained by preserving it while also sharing it through open access. To address many societal challenges faced by South Africa, decision makers in government and private sector need to be the first customers for modelling and simulation technologies.



Mandate: • Develop and provide open data sharing. • Promote and support open data policy. • Establishing and advocating collaboration network that promote modelling and simulation techniques in academic institutions and industries. • Raise the standards of best-practice in the modelling and simulation. • Encourage innovation and industry-related skills while also promoting modelling and simulation.
